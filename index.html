Technical Expectations

Things That Happen (And What to Do)

These 4 situations are normal in automation. Here's what they look like and what we need from you.

Meta Platform Outages

Meta (Instagram/Facebook) occasionally goes down or changes APIs. Automations may pause temporarily.

⚡ Your Action: Nothing — we monitor and fix it

Permission Drops

Sometimes Instagram disconnects our access. You'll need to re-authorize when we send a link.

⚡ Your Action: Re-authorize access when prompted

Pre-Existing Automations

If you already have automations running, they can conflict with ours. Let us know about any active tools.

⚡ Your Action: Tell us about existing tools

New Custom Builds

When you request something new, it takes time to spec, build, and test. Turnaround depends on complexity.

⚡ Your Action: Submit requests via Slack
Urgency Guide

When to Worry (And When Not To)

Not every glitch is an emergency. Here's how to tell the difference.

✓ Don't Worry

  • Automation paused for a few hours during Meta outage
  • A single DM didn't send (edge case, auto-retries)
  • Slight delay in CRM sync after a platform update
  • You see a "reconnect" prompt — just re-auth
  • We notify you proactively about a known issue

⚠ Reach Out

  • Automations have been down for 24+ hours with no update
  • Wrong messages are being sent to your audience
  • You're getting complaints from followers about DMs
  • A funnel is live but not capturing any leads
  • Something looks broken right before a launch or campaign
Issue Reporting

How to Report Tech Issues

The faster and clearer your report, the faster we fix it. Use this framework.

Where

Which Platform?

Instagram DMs, Stories, Comment tool, CRM, email — tell us exactly where.

What

What Happened?

Describe what you saw vs. what you expected. Screenshots help a lot.

When

When Did It Start?

Approximate time and date. Did it happen once or is it ongoing?

Drop it in Slack with these 3 details and we'll handle it from there.
Example Reports

What a Great Report Looks Like

See the difference between vague and clear issue reports.

✗ Vague

"Hey, the bot's not working."

✓ Clear

"The new follower DM funnel on Instagram stopped sending since yesterday around 3 PM. I tested with a new follow and didn't get a message. Here's a screenshot."
Diagnosing and fixing the issue is our job. You don't need to figure out why it broke. Just tell us what you're seeing, where, and when — we take it from there.
