Technical Expectations

Things That Happen (And What to Do)

Our team handles these behind the scenes so you can stay focused on your business.

Meta Platform Outages

Meta (Instagram/Facebook) occasionally goes down or changes APIs. Automations may pause temporarily.

⚡ Our team monitors the situation and ensures automations resume once the platform stabilizes

Permission Drops

Occasionally platform permissions disconnect. Our team monitors and reconnects these proactively. In most cases no action is required from you.

⚡ Your Action: Usually none. We monitor this proactively and handle it before you even notice.

New Funnel Requests

When a new funnel request is submitted, our team reviews the scope and schedules it into the build pipeline.

⚡ Your Action: Bring it up in Slack or mention it on your next call. We'll capture what was discussed, scope it out, and add it to the build queue.
Urgency Guide

When to Worry (And When Not To)

We've got eyes on everything. Here's when to relax and when to flag something for us.

✓ No Action Required

  • Meta platform outages (automations resume automatically)
  • Temporary DM delays (edge cases auto-retry)
  • Minor CRM sync delays after a platform update

⚠ Please Notify Us

  • Incorrect messages are being sent to your audience
  • A funnel is behaving unexpectedly or not capturing leads
  • Something looks wrong during a launch or campaign
Issue Reporting

How to Report Tech Issues

Our team moves fast — the clearer the report, the faster the fix. Use this simple framework.

Where

Which Platform?

Instagram DMs, Story replies, Comment tool, email platform, or CRM — tell us exactly where.

What

What Happened?

Describe what you saw vs. what you expected. Screenshots help a lot.

When

When Did It Start?

Approximate time and date. Did it happen once or is it ongoing?

Drop it in Slack with these 3 details and we'll handle it from there.
Example Reports

What a Great Report Looks Like

See the difference between vague and clear issue reports.

✗ Vague

"Hey, the bot's not working."

✓ Clear

"The new follower DM funnel on Instagram stopped sending since yesterday around 3 PM. I tested with a new follow and didn't get a message. Here's a screenshot."
Diagnosing and fixing the issue is our job. You don't need to figure out why it broke. Just tell us what you're seeing, where, and when. We'll take it from there.
