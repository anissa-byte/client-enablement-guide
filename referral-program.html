The Math

What this actually looks like on your bill.

Referral discounts stack. The more people you send, the lower your bill gets.

Example: Your bill with active referrals

Your base subscription $15,000/mo
1 active referral —$1,000/mo
2 active referrals —$2,000/mo
3 active referrals —$3,000/mo
3 referrals active = you pay $12,000/mo

Each referral discount runs for up to 6 months from the date your referral signs. If a referred client leaves before 6 months, the discount for that referral ends at that time. Discounts are applied as a credit to your subscription — not paid out in cash.

Questions

Everything else you might want to know.

When does my discount start?

Your discount is applied to your subscription as soon as your referral's first payment clears. You don't need to do anything — we apply it automatically.

How long does it run?

Up to 6 months per referral, starting from the date your referral signs. If they're still a client after 6 months, the reward wraps at that point. If they leave earlier, it ends when they do.

Can I refer more than one person?

Yes — there's no limit. Each active referral adds $1,000/month to your discount. Some clients have referred enough people to bring their bill to $0.

Do I get anything if my referral goes to coaching instead of DFY?

The referral reward applies to DFY (Done-For-You) clients only. If someone you introduce ends up in a coaching program, we appreciate the intro but the reward doesn't apply.

Can my referral get a discount too?

The referral discount applies to your subscription as the referrer. Your contact signs at standard pricing — but they get access to the same results that made you a client worth referring from.

What if I'm not sure if someone is a fit?

Send them to us anyway — especially if they have the audience. We'd rather do a quick call to find out than have you second-guess a good intro. Even if the timing isn't right, we'll keep them warm.

How do I know if my discount is applied?

You'll see the updated amount on your next invoice. If you ever have questions about your referral status, message Anissa in Slack.
